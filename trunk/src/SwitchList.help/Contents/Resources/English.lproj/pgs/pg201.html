Changing Default Fonts in SwitchList

Some folks might not like the default font choices for switchlists, either because of size of typeface. The easiest way to change font sizes (or switchlist styles) is to design your own switchlist format. The SwitchList development site has details on how to create your own switchlists and other reports, and customize them to match your needs.

The Thomas the Tank Engine switchlist example shows what can be done with a small bit of work.

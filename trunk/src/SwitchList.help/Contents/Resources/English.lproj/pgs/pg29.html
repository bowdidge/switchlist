Preferences Window

SwitchList-wide preferences are set from the Preferences window, which can be displayed by choosing the "Preferences..." menu item in the SwitchList menu.

Preferences controls the kind of switch list that is displayed, and also controls whether the "Switchlists on the iPad" feature is enabled.

To change the kind of switchlists displayed, click the button next to the switchlist you want. Any switchlists you create after this point will use the new form.

Definition: Offline Town

Towns represent major areas on your layout, and help your train crew know where they're going. If the location doesn't exist and trains can't reach it, then you can mark a town as "offline". Offline towns are special because trains can never be directed as running through those towns, and a freight car reaching staging but destined for an industry in an offline town is assumed to have arrived.
